💡 Why Chemistry?

Research Philosophy

My earliest chemistry projects focused on extraction, synthesis, and formulation. Over time, I became fascinated by a broader question: why do certain materials survive across centuries while others disappear? That question led me beyond the laboratory and into Vietnamese cultural heritage. Silk garments, calligraphy, herbal medicines, and traditional dyes are not only cultural artifacts; they are material systems shaped by chemistry, environment, and human craftsmanship. I now approach chemistry as a bridge between scientific inquiry and cultural preservation — studying how molecular properties, pigments, fibers, and bioactive compounds can help us understand and sustain Vietnamese traditions in a modern world.
